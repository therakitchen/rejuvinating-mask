REJUVINATING GEL MASK: For Oily of Dry Skin

Our products are personally crafted and handmade by Thera Kitchen.

Keeping the skin healthy is the key to get a dewy and plumped skin which is our straight-forward goal for a 'woke-up like this' look. Using makeup aesthetically improves the physical appearance of the skin but to create beautiful makeup creations, a flawless healthy skin is responsible for the 90% of your over-all look. This Rejuvinating Gel Mask can help you achieve this goal with consistent use. Depending on how your current skin's health was when you started using this sleeping mask, but for standard individuals, you can visibly see the effect after 3 days. 

Our main active ingredients were Oat Extract and Niacinamide. Oats can gently exfoliate the skin while protecting & strengthening the skin barrier that's why it's the best ingredient for both oily and dry skin. Rich with beta-glucans, it can effectively moisturize, gives a soothing relief, reduces inflammation and supports wound healing. Oat extracts or sometimes known as Avena Sativa is rich with Vitamins B1, B2, B3 B6 and E. Most importantly, it has a unique phenolic compound called Avenanthramides which have strong anti-inflammatory and antixidant properties.

Niacinamide on the other hand is a versatile skincare ingredient with multiple benefits. Popularly known to reduce hyperpigmentation and lighten dark spots, it can also provide anti-inflammatory and anti-aging effects by promoting collagen production that also improves skin elasticity. Keeping the skin hydrated, niacinamide regulates oil production and prevents water loss that it enhances the skin's moisture content for a dewy plump skin.

FOR OILY SKIN, we added moringa and tea tree oils. Moringa is rich with Vit A for skin renewal, Vit C for collagen production and Vit E to protect the skin. Consists with zinc, it can effectively heal wounds and control acne. Moringa also have a natural abundant amount of anti-oxidants and fatty acids omega 3,6 and 9, that can further repair, regenerate and strengthen the skin barrier. Tea Tree is a popular botanical active for acne-prone skin because of its multiple key components that is known to have strong anti-microbial, anti-fungal & anti-inflammatory properties.

FOR DRY SKIN, we added aloe vera and chamomile extracts. Aloe Vera promotes skin repair and faster turnover that it helps with skin damage and aging. Rich with enzymes that breaks down sugars and fats, it can also promote gentle exfoliation of dead skin cells while giving a soothing relief from inflammations. Chamomile on the other hand is rich with flavonoids that soothes and protects dry irritated skin. Consists of terpenoids and polysaccharides, it's an effective anti-inflammatory ingredient that hydrates, locks-in moisture and creates a protective skin barrier against free radical damage and other environmental stressors.
